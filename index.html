FEATURES

Constant Battle is currently based in the Triad, NC and was founded after hearing one veteran's words: "I'm fighting for my life here at home, man. My life's like a constant battle." It functions to examine the reality of veteran life in face with the universal respect veterans receive. Here are the stories of everyday veterans.

SERVICES

Constant Battle's mission is to help connect veterans to all the resources available in the area, along with gaining attention for their stories. These are services involved in providing advocacy for housing, free college courses, and more to those who need these things in the area.
