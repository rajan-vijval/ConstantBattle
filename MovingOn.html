STORIES

"I was ins the military for some time. I was active duty around Desert Storm. I worked as an electrician some, and worked in the National Guard. I had to leave because I started working on my "captain's qualifications" too early. They require a certain neck to waist ratio, and my neck's always been a little small, ha-ha.

I started doing different jobs after I got out. I drove a cab for some time up North, got a job in a textile plant, worked at Walrmart, and some other places. I remember one place I worked at was a for-profit organization that also donated to the military. That was interesting. One of my bosses there wasn't a good guy - he was using whatever money we brought in for drugs. I got laid off because his numbers weren't adding up.

Now I'm here. I'm waiting to move in to my new apartment, and have a job lined up. I'm excited. They just need to move the furniture in. I'll have a job and be ready to move on."
