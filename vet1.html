STORIES

"I want to be a history teacher. I have an master's degree in history and plenty of experience. But no one will hire me. Maybe it's because I need a walker to get around or maybe it's because I don't have any teeth. Or maybe I'm too old.

I'm 83 and I served in the Korean War. Sometimes I stay with my sister or sometimes I stay at my house. But I need to find somewhere else to live. Somewhere cheaper.

I get social security but that's all the money I get. I haven't been able to find work for years. I don't have any teeth. I need dentures but they're not covered by the VA. I had my teeth pulled at a charity event but it turns out I need another surgery to straighten the knots in my gums. It's not covered either. I can't walk without a walker on account of my back. I need surgery to align my spinal disks but it's not covered. They keep giving me medication. I don't know why they don't just give me surgery.

I was a high school English teacher a long time ago. You could say I retired. I had to leave because the students got too rough for me. They started bringing knives and weapons in to class. Now I just save money the best I can. I thought I'd come here for the free food. It's my first time. The VA wants to put me into assisted living but I hope it won't come to that."
