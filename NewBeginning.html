STORIES

"I'm 55 years old and I'm excited for my new beginning.

I've had my ups and downs. I've been homeless a lot these past few years, but didn't know about all the resources I had access to. A lot of these guys complain about the VA, but I never bothered with them. I'm a veteran from a time of peace, so I didn't know they could help me out. But when they found me homeless, they got me a social worker and helped me find this place. I have a bed, 3 hot meals a day, and help with transitioning into a place of work and a home.

I'm ready to leave; real excited to start a new life. I've worked as a mechanic in the past, and am looking for new jobs in the area. I'm getting my own place, and my benefits are helping me pay for it.

I've never been addicted to drugs or alcohol. Not everybody who ends up homeless makes all bad decisions. It's rough out here, man. I've just been through some tough times. But with God's grace, I'm still here today, about to start my new life. I could stay here for up to two years but I'm ready to go now. I'm happy to wake up every morning and face the day."
